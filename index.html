About The Serrum

Serrum enables the world's first decentralized NFTs Token, Serrum, built on Binance Smart Chain that is backed by a basket of NFTs and crypto assets without centralized control.

Decentralized

Access an immutable money market protocol directly on-chain.

BEP-20

All Serrum Protocol assets are bound by the BEP-20 standard.

Scalable

Built on Binance Smart Chain for fast, secure, and low cost.

Savings

Earn interest on your assets
Funds held within the protocol can earn APY's based on the market demand for that asset. Interest is earned by the block and can be used as collateral to borrow assets or to mint stablecoins.

Protocol

Money Markets built on Binance Smart Chain You can now tokenize your assets utilizing the Binance Smart Chain and receive portable Serrum that you can freely move around to cold storage, transfer to other users, and more.

HOW TO PURCHASE SERRUM

01. Download MetaMask or Trust Wallet Applications.
02. Purchase Binance Tokens (BNB) and send them to your MetaMask or Trust Wallet.
03. On the home page of SERRUM click on "Purchase Tokens" button. This will open a Dapp Exchange Called "PancakeSwap".
04. A popup will open, click the check box "I understand", this will load SERRUM into PancakeSwap.
05. Make sure your MetaMask Wallet or Trust Wallet Is synced with PancakeSwap.
